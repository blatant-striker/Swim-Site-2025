Registration Process

After submitting your registration form, you will receive a confirmation email within 24-48 hours with details about the next steps, including payment information and orientation schedule.

New swimmers may be required to attend an assessment session to determine the appropriate program placement.

Fees & Payment

Program fees vary depending on the selected program and duration. Payment can be made via credit card, e-transfer, or check.

A non-refundable registration fee of $50 is required to secure your spot.

Code of Conduct

All WEST swimmers and families agree to:

  • Demonstrate respect for coaches, officials, volunteers, and fellow swimmers at all times
  • Promote good sportsmanship and team spirit
  • Attend practices regularly and arrive on time
  • Follow all pool rules and safety guidelines
  • Represent WEST positively at competitions and in the community
  • Refrain from bullying, harassment, or discriminatory behavior
  • Respect pool facilities and equipment
  • Communicate respectfully with coaches regarding concerns
  • Support team fundraising efforts and volunteer activities
  • Comply with all Swim Ontario and Swimming Canada codes of conduct

Violations of this code may result in disciplinary action, including but not limited to: verbal warning, written warning, temporary suspension from team activities, or dismissal from the team without refund of fees.

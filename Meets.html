Meet Information

For all meets listed on our calendar, please note the following important information:

  • Meet registration forms must be completed by the posted deadlines
  • Meet fees will be charged to your account upon registration
  • Parents must fulfill officiating duties assigned for home meets
  • Swimmers should arrive at least 15 minutes before scheduled warm-up times
  • All swimmers must wear WEST team caps and apparel at competitions

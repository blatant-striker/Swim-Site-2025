WEST Accomplishments

A legacy of excellence in competitive swimming

  • Developed World Record Holder Kylie Masse and Amanda Reason
  • WEST Swimmers have Won Medals at the Olympic Games
  • WEST Alumna Kylie Masse becomes first Canadian swimmer (2024) to win an individual medal at three Olympics
  • WEST Swimmers have Attained 10 Canadian National Records and 24 Ontario Records
  • WEST has produced 253 Ontario Provincial Champions Since 2002
  • Eastern Canadian National Champions
  • Masters Provincial, National and World Champions
  • WEST has produced World Cup Finalists
  • Over 27 WEST Swimmers have received Full Athletic Scholarships
  • Division 1 Swim Ontario Team Champions
  • Winter Provincials 2023 Most Improved Male Team
  • 2023 Team Showdown Champions

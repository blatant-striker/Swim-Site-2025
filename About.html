Our Mission

Windsor Essex Swim Team provides a safe, fun, and challenging experience for every swimmer. We focus on shared experiences and believe that long-term, successful development takes time and requires collaboration between the coach, swimmer, and support system.

The WEST Approach

Our approach focuses on small group instruction, ensuring that no athlete gets left behind. We believe in developing the whole athlete - not just their swimming technique, but their character, confidence, and life skills.

With over 100 years of combined coaching experience, our staff is dedicated to maximizing each swimmer's potential through personalized training programs and mentorship.

Championship Success

Our athletes have achieved remarkable success at all levels of competition, from local meets to Olympic medals. We celebrate each swimmer's individual journey and achievements.

WEST swimmers have represented Canada in international competitions and earned countless medals at provincial and national championships.

Why Join WEST Swimming

Our core values guide everything we do and shape the experience for every swimmer who joins our team.

Opportunity

No matter your experience level or skill, WEST provides the opportunity to reach your potential through personalized coaching and progressive training.

Athlete Development

We develop athletes emotionally, physically, and mentally through holistic training programs tailored to their age and experience level.

Integrity

WEST athletes learn to conduct themselves with integrity both inside and outside of the pool, building character that lasts a lifetime.

Fun

We don't do negative and believe every athlete should have fun. Our coaches provide an inclusive and enjoyable experience for all swimmers.

Success

WEST swimmers have competed in Olympic Games, National competitions, Provincial championships, and earned athletic scholarships across North America.

Confidence

Success in the pool equals confidence outside of the pool. These are skills and abilities that will benefit athletes throughout their lives.

Our Facilities

Train in world-class facilities designed to help you reach your swimming goals.

Competition Pool

50-meter Olympic-standard pool with 8 lanes, perfect for training and competitions. Features state-of-the-art timing systems and spectator seating.

Training Pool

25-meter training pool ideal for technique work, warm-ups, and smaller group sessions. Adjustable depth for all skill levels.

WFCU Centre

Modern aquatic facilities at the WFCU Centre providing additional training opportunities in a professional environment.

Benefits of Officiating

Becoming a WEST official offers numerous benefits and helps support our swimmers' competitive journey.

  • Be part of your child's swimming experience
  • Learn the rules and technicalities of competitive swimming
  • Support the club and meet your volunteer requirements
  • Develop skills that can lead to provincial and national level officiating
  • Be part of the swimming community in a meaningful way

Training Advantages

WEST swimmers benefit from training in some of the best aquatic facilities in Ontario, providing the perfect environment for developing champions.

  • Multiple pool options across Windsor-Essex County
  • Olympic-standard equipment and timing systems
  • Dedicated dryland training spaces
  • Video analysis capability for technique improvement
  • Access to specialized training tools and equipment
